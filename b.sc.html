Information B.sc




A Bachelor of Science (BS, BSc, SB, or ScB; from the Latin baccalaureus scientiae or scientiae baccalaureus)[1] is a bachelor's degree awarded for programs that generally last three to five years.[2] The first university to admit a student to the degree of Bachelor of Science was the University of London in 1860.[3] Whether Bachelor of Science or Bachelor of Arts degrees are awarded in particular subjects varies between universities.
