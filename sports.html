KHO-KHO

Neither ball nor bat is required in the game of kho-kho. For this, only 111 feet long and 51 feet wide ground is required. Leaving a space of ten feet each and four feet high, two wooden poles are buried and the distance between these pillars is divided into eight equal parts in such a way that the players of both the teams face each other in the direction. Face to face, they sit in their respective places. Each team is given seven minutes for each innings and within the stipulated time that team has to finish its innings.
